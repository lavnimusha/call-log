Make sense of your call data

Call Log Analytics is a smart analytics app that lets you analyze your call data. This is the first app of its kind that offers an integrated experience of Analytics, Dialer, Calls Usage, to track your call billing, and unlimited call log backup.

Analytics

View analytics of your call data by duration and frequency. Filter by date range, outgoing and incoming calls, etc.

Dialer

Use Call Log Analytics as default phone app for your incoming and outgoing calls, and view analytics for each number.

Backup

Never lose your call log. Automatically backup your call log to Google Drive. You can restore when you install the app.
